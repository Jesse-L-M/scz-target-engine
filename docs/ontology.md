# Initial Ontology

This file defines the initial disease-domain and illness-stage vocabulary for the repo. `v0` does not separately score any of these categories yet.

- Acute positive symptoms: short-horizon reduction of hallucinations, delusions, and related acute psychotic symptoms. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- Relapse prevention: reducing recurrence risk after stabilization or remission. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- Treatment-resistant schizophrenia: schizophrenia that remains inadequately controlled despite standard antipsychotic exposure. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- Clozapine-resistant schizophrenia: residual illness burden despite adequate clozapine treatment. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- Negative symptoms: deficits such as avolition, anhedonia, alogia, blunted affect, and social withdrawal. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- Cognition: effects on attention, working memory, executive function, and related neurocognitive performance. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- CHR / transition prevention: reducing transition from clinical high risk states into threshold psychotic illness. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
- Functioning / durable recovery relevance: effects on real-world functioning, sustained recovery, and longitudinal quality-of-life relevance. `v0` does not score this separately yet.
